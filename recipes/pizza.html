Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

This is the recipe for a pepperoni pizza

Description

These are the ingredients you will need to create a pepperoni pizza! For step-by-step cooking instructions see details below.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Take your fresh dough, flatten it and give it a nice round shape.
  3. Add shredded cheese and pepperoni.
  4. Cook the pizza for 18 - 25 minutes. Be sure to check on it when it's close to done so you don't let it burn!
